Three days

of snow
so rare for London
it feels cozier now inside,
you rearranged the furniture
and my parents' package finally came
big in a box, tights for me, Calvin Klein and
for you, very fitted sweats
thanks mom
she always had great taste

I find congratulation cards, all from the heart
from my family for our wedding
so happy we could be with you virtually
i could cry of happiness and the precipice of new time
of Royal Mail in covid, 6-8 weeks
bless you, it has arrived
