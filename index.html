First time is best (February)

Snowdrops popped up under the neighbor's tree, I think
We can steal a few
put them in our own garden out front
it's not ours, it's rented
but it is infront of us, nonetheless
Good idea.

rain comes down. "welcome to England"
I have never written beside you before
well, a few things unprecedented, so far
for 2021
to say the least.

One month ago BoJo said, we are locking in, locking down,
a third time
and this will be a long haul
It took me time, I admit, to feel it
there is joy, still, in all the days
and they move, now, out of time

Will you let the quiet in
it feels like death, so no
but it's just there, waiting
had I been chasing whatever dreams, i didn't even know it
years and years, great band
to unlearn it now, when the most important part of life has been ordered
halt

He said, "do you miss Canada?"
I said my friends and family
It is hard to be away
now a weight felt by more, I suppose
slowed days have made
my bustling feel silly
acclimatized patterns, fizzle
time of day for this, that, tit for tat

I wonder, where were they even born?
an answer comes,
"under pressure"

plants grow greener
I want more
the world is this house,
become a jungle
where we can prowl
awake, dream and make
into all of the day and night's hours
what is real
quiet grows louder
and it has been here,
all along
